These files contain data from the CDC which will be used to analyze whether certain pre-existing conditions increase chances of covid morbidity.

Diabetes Source:  
Center for Disease Control. (2020, August 28). National Diabetes Statistics Report, 2020. Retrieved December 04, 2020, from https://www.cdc.gov/diabetes/data/statistics-report/index.html
This source provides data on the percentage of people of various age ranges have diabetes. This data will be useful to find the infection rate per week of COVID-19. We will use this to compare the fatality rate of people suffering from pre-existing conditions compared to healthy people
